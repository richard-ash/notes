# Sociology

Cultural evolution, social structure, demographic dynamics, and the mechanisms by which group behaviors propagate, drift, and select.

## Articles
- [[cultural-drift-maladaptation]] — Hanson's argument that humanity has broken its cultural-evolution-by-natural-selection engine: collapse of cultural variety into a monoculture, near-elimination of selection pressure (war/famine/disease), and acceleration of environmental change and activist-driven drift have moved key parameters into a region where cultural species-defining features should drift into maladaptation; differential fertility is the only remaining selection mechanism, leaving insular fertile subcultures (Amish, Haredim) as the projected demographic inheritors
