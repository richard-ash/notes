The forensic move is "the prior is not the posterior" — concede the prior (Varma works through and rejects the standard speed-of-light and civilizational-coherence objections, agreeing with Cowen that sub-light self-replicating probes can fill the galaxy in 10⁶–10⁷ years and that the dandelion / distributed-civilization models defeat the coherence objection), then crush the posterior via the likelihood term. The physics core is a flyby/rendezvous dichotomy: a 0.1c flyby crosses the inner solar system in hours (Breakthrough Starshot is explicitly this design) so it is a streak, not a sighting; rendezvous requires either Tsiolkovsky-killer mass ratios (fusion drive 0.05c exhaust, 0.2c delta-v → e⁴ ≈ 55 mass ratio → 98% fuel) or external momentum transfer (solar radiation pressure 9 µN/m² → ~km² sail areas) — and even Heller-Hippke's ultralight Starshot-rendezvous design at Proxima requires 76 g sail, 10 g payload, 10⁵ m² sail area ("more stadium than spacecraft") and tops out at 4.6%c not 10%c, with the common pattern across every rendezvous scheme being ultralight-payload + huge-apparatus + long-baseline — incompatible with compact atmospheric loitering. The kinetic-energy framing — 100 kg at 0.1c carries 4.5 × 10¹⁶ J ≈ 10 megatons ≈ 700 Hiroshimas, slow braking trades a 140 MW power bill for ~half a light-year (tens of thousands of AU) of stopping distance — is the rhetorical anchor: "a bullet trying to stop inside a room without hitting anything" requires gel the size of the room, and the gel becomes the story. The slow-probe escape (drop cruise speed to 0.01c or 0.001c → KE drops 100× or 10000×, Tsiolkovsky relaxes) is granted — but moves the evidentiary burden to archaeology: self-replicating Barlow-style probes on million-to-billion-year timescales should produce persistent infrastructure (processed asteroids, factories, waste heat, communication nodes, spectral anomalies) that fuzzy atmospheric orbs are not; the "lurker parked since the Pliocene" sub-hypothesis is physically allowed but produces orbital/thermal/spectral signatures, *not* low-altitude videos, so it is a genuinely different hypothesis from the atmospheric-sightings hypothesis and evidence for one is not evidence for the other; the "probes build local factories from asteroid materials" sub-hypothesis adds a second Fermi paradox inside the first (if they built factories, where are the factories?).
