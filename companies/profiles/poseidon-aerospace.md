# Poseidon Aerospace

## Overview
Building autonomous unmanned cargo aircraft to revolutionize air logistics by lowering cost per flight-ton-mile.

## Notes
- Three aircraft platforms: Seagull (unmanned surface and aerial seaplane), Heron (unmanned seaplane), Egret (unmanned fixed wing)
- Focus on making air logistics as affordable and flexible as possible
- Reviving Cold War-era "sea monster" glider technology
- $11M raised November 2025
